## 1. Executive Summary

The Alpha Pilot program targets **3-5 high-quality partners** to validate the platform's core value proposition ("Visual Truth"). This document defines the selection criteria, pitch, and success metrics for this cohort.

---

## 2. Ideal Alpha Profile (IAP)

We are **NOT** looking for giant enterprises yet (sales cycle too long). We want agile mid-market players with a specific pain point.

### Criteria
- **Segment:** Mid-Market P&C Carrier or Innovative TPA.
- **Tech Stack:** Modern (Cloud-native, API-friendly).
- **Pain Point:** High "Soft Fraud" rates (e.g., photo manipulation in auto claims).
- **Champion:** VP of Claims or Head of Innovation who can authorize a pilot.

### Target List (Example Types)
1.  **"Tech-First Auto Insurer"** (e.g., Root, Metromile style).
2.  **"Gig Economy Insurer"** (e.g., ride-share insurance provider).
3.  **"Claims Automation Vendor"** (Partnership, not customer).

---

## 3. The Alpha Offer

**"Join the Proveniq Trust Circle."**

### What they get:
- **Early Access:** First to use the immutable ledger for fraud defense.
- **White Glove Support:** Dedicated Solutions Engineer (Slack channel).
- **Pricing:** Heavily discounted (or free for first 6 months) in exchange for a Case Study.
- **Influence:** Direct line to Product roadmap.

### What we ask:
- **Usage:** Minimum 1,000 claims/month in Sandbox → Prod.
- **Feedback:** Bi-weekly product calls.
- **Marketing:** Public logo usage and Case Study upon success.
